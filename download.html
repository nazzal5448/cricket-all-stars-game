How to Download Cricket All Stars on Android

The game is currently live on the Google Play Store for Android devices in India. Here is how to get it installed in under five minutes.

Availability — Where Can You Download It?

Cricket All Stars is published by KRAFTON, Inc. and is officially available on the Google Play Store. The game is in early access as of May 2025 and is currently restricted to Android devices in India.

There is no iOS version and no confirmed date for a global rollout. If you are outside India, the game will not appear in your regional Play Store listing. We will update this page when that changes.

Step-by-Step Download Guide

The safest and easiest way to install Cricket All Stars is directly from the Google Play Store. Follow these steps:

1

Open the Google Play Store

Tap the Play Store icon on your Android home screen or app drawer. Make sure you are signed into your Google account.

2

Search for Cricket All Stars

Type "Cricket All Stars Krafton" in the search bar. This is more specific than just "Cricket All Stars" and gets you to the right game faster — there are a few games with similar names.

3

Check the Developer Name

On the game listing, confirm the developer name shows as KRAFTON, Inc. before tapping Install. The package name is com.krafton.cricketallstars.

4

Tap Install

Tap the Install button. The download is 356 MB — use Wi-Fi if you are on a limited mobile data plan. Download time on a standard 4G connection is around 30 to 60 seconds.

5

Open and Play

Once installed, tap Open from the Play Store or find the game icon in your app drawer. The first launch needs an internet connection for initial setup. After that, offline modes work without data.

Should You Download the APK?

Third-party sites like APKPure and Uptodown host Cricket All Stars APK files. The APKPure version (0.1.0) has been verified by VirusTotal and is listed as safe. The file is the same 356 MB package.

That said, the Play Store version is always the recommended route. It receives automatic updates, is linked to your Google account, and has no installation permission issues. APK files from third-party sources require you to enable installs from unknown sources in your Android settings, which opens up a small security risk from other apps.

Only use a third-party APK if you have a specific reason — for example, if the game is not showing in your region's Play Store or if a Play Store update is delayed.

Download Troubleshooting

The game is not appearing in the Play Store

Cricket All Stars is currently geo-restricted to India. If you are outside India, the game will not appear in your regional listing. Some users have reported success using a VPN to an India server, but this is not officially supported by KRAFTON.

Not enough storage space

The APK file is 356 MB but you need roughly 1.5 GB total free space for the game to install and run properly. Clear unused apps, photos, or downloaded files to free up space before installing.

Installation failed

If the install fails, restart your device and try again from the Play Store. If you previously installed an APK version from another source, uninstall it first before installing from the Play Store — conflicting signatures cause install failures.

Game crashes on launch

This most often happens on devices with less than 3 GB RAM or Android below version 6.0. Close all background apps before launching. If the issue persists, clear the app cache from Settings > Apps > Cricket All Stars > Clear Cache.

The game is in early access — what does that mean?

Early access means the game is live but not fully released. KRAFTON is collecting feedback to fix bugs and add content before the full launch. You may encounter minor issues. Any progress you make during early access should carry over to the full release, but KRAFTON has not made a formal confirmation on that yet.
