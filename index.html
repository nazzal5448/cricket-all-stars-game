About the Game

What Is Cricket All Stars?

Cricket All Stars is a mobile cricket game developed and published by KRAFTON, Inc. — the South Korean gaming company behind PUBG: Battlegrounds and Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI). The game soft-launched on Android in India in May 2025 and is currently in early access.

Unlike simulation-heavy titles like Real Cricket or WCC3, Cricket All Stars goes for a faster, arcade-first approach. The controls are tap-friendly, the pacing is quick, and the whole experience is wrapped in broadcast-quality visuals that look more like a live TV feed than a mobile game.

The game is free to download on the Google Play Store for Android devices running Android 6.0 or higher. It has in-app purchases but does not require spending to enjoy the core game modes.

This is also KRAFTON's second cricket title — the company took over publishing rights for Real Cricket in late 2024. Cricket All Stars signals a much bigger push into India's cricket gaming market.

Key Features

What Makes This Game Different

📺

Broadcast-Style Visuals

Every match runs at 60 FPS with cinematic camera angles that replicate the feel of watching cricket on TV. Stadium lighting, crowd reactions, and slow-motion replays are all here.

🏏

Licensed IPL Teams

Play as Kolkata Knight Riders or Lucknow Super Giants with official jerseys and real player likenesses. More IPL teams are expected once the game exits early access.

👆

Tap-Friendly Controls

No steep learning curve. The batting system is built around timing and direction — you read the delivery, pick your shot, and tap. The bowling feedback system shows ball trajectory in real time.

🎮

5 Game Modes

Quick Match, Friendly Match, Test Match, Indian Pro T20 League, and 20-Over World Cup. More modes including an ODI World Cup and USA T20 League are confirmed for future updates.

🏟️

Stadium Customisation

Choose your wickets, fireworks, flags, and laser effects before the match. Daytime and floodlit night matches are both available.

📶

Offline Mode Available

Play the T20 League and World Cup modes without an internet connection. Good for commuters and anyone on patchy mobile data.

Game Modes

All 5 Modes Explained

01

Quick Match

Jump into a short format match instantly. No setup, no team selection screens. Good for five-minute sessions when you just want to bat.

02

Friendly Match

Challenge a friend or set up a custom match with your preferred teams and settings. Works over an internet connection.

03

Test Match

The traditional five-day format brought to mobile. Patience is required but the strategy depth is worth it for hardcore fans.

04

Indian Pro T20 League

Pick your IPL team and grind through the full T20 league season. Available offline. Choose your difficulty from Amateur up to Legendary.

05

20-Over World Cup

Represent a national team in the T20 World Cup format. Also offline-capable. Ideal for players who prefer international cricket over the IPL.
